About Shiba Matcha

Shiba Matcha is a Brisbane-based company dedicated to bringing the finest Japanese matcha to Australia. We source our matcha directly from Uji, Japan, ensuring the highest quality and authenticity. Our mission is to share the rich tradition and health benefits of matcha with our customers. We value every customer and strive to provide exceptional service and premium products. Whether you're a matcha enthusiast or new to this vibrant green tea, we invite you to explore our offerings and experience the true essence of Japanese matcha.
